    Malaria

    Malaria is transmitted through bites of infected mosquitos. Common symtoms include: fever,headache, fatigue, cough, diarrhea...

    To prevent contacting it, use mosquito nets to sleep, take vaccines and apply mosquito repellants in closed rooms at least twice weekly.

    Typhoid

    Typhoid is often caught by eating or drinking contaminated food or water. Common symptoms are headache, diarrhea, fever, stomach ache...

    Preventions include vaccination, avoid untreated water and eating raw fruits and vegetables without washing.
